Making Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 ounces/400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces/170g) tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups (500g) ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup (50g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

How to make it!

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic and cook until the onion is softened.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to blend.
  4. While the sauce is simmering, cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine the ricotta cheese and half of the shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Place three lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, slightly overlapping them.
  7. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by a third of the remaining meat sauce. Repeat this layering process with three more noodles, the remaining ricotta cheese mixture, and another third of the meat sauce.
  8. Top with the final layer of three lasagna noodles and spread the remaining meat sauce over the top. Sprinkle the remaining shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese on top.
  9. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Once cooked, remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil leaves, if desired.

Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
